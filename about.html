About Ben Tours

Discover our passion for authentic Sri Lankan adventures

Our Story

Founded with a vision to share Sri Lanka's incredible natural beauty with the world, Ben Tours has been creating authentic, eco-friendly travel experiences for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts.

We began as a small team of passionate local guides and naturalists who believed that Sri Lanka's pristine wilderness, ancient heritage, and warm hospitality deserved to be experienced responsibly. Today, we specialize in sustainable tourism that protects our island's natural treasures while supporting local communities.

Every journey we craft is a testament to our commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and exceptional service. We don't just show you Sri Lanka; we help you feel its heartbeat.

Our Mission

To create transformative eco-adventures that connect travelers with Sri Lanka's pristine nature and authentic culture while preserving our island's natural heritage for future generations.

Promote sustainable and responsible tourism practices
Protect wildlife and natural habitats through conservation tourism
Support local communities and traditional livelihoods
Create authentic connections between travelers and local culture
