Cultural Triangle

4 Days of Incredible Wildlife Encounters

Wildlife Paradise

Experience Sri Lanka's incredible wildlife with leopard spotting in Yala, elephant encounters in Udawalawe, and bird watching in Sinharaja.

Sri Lanka is a wildlife enthusiast's paradise, home to leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and over 400 bird species. This safari adventure takes you through the island's premier national parks, offering the best opportunities to witness these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

Duration

4 Days / 3 Nights

Group Size

Nature Lovers

Price

$749.00

4-Day Wildlife Safari Itinerary

Your journey through Sri Lanka's wild kingdom

1

Arrival & Yala National Park

Begin your wildlife adventure at Sri Lanka's most famous national park, home to the highest density of leopards in the world.

  • Airport pickup and transfer to Yala region
  • Check-in at safari lodge near Yala
  • Afternoon game drive in Yala National Park
  • Leopard spotting and wildlife photography
  • Sunset viewing at Patanangala Beach
  • Evening wildlife briefing with naturalist
  • Overnight stay near Yala
2

Full Day Yala Safari

Spend a full day exploring Yala's diverse ecosystems, from dry forests to coastal lagoons.

  • Early morning game drive (best time for leopards)
  • Breakfast in the wilderness
  • Visit to ancient Sithulpawwa Rock Temple
  • Afternoon safari in different park zones
  • Spot elephants, sloth bears, and crocodiles
  • Bird watching at Menik Ganga
  • Overnight stay near Yala
3

Udawalawe Elephant Park

Journey to Udawalawe, famous for its large elephant herds and the Elephant Transit Home.

  • Morning departure to Udawalawe
  • Visit Elephant Transit Home feeding time
  • Afternoon game drive in Udawalawe National Park
  • Observe large elephant herds
  • Water buffalo and bird watching
  • Photography at Udawalawe Reservoir
  • Overnight stay near Udawalawe
4

Sinharaja Rainforest & Departure

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Sinharaja Rainforest, home to endemic species and incredible biodiversity.

  • Early morning departure to Sinharaja
  • Guided nature walk in the rainforest
  • Endemic bird watching with expert guide
  • Spot purple-faced langurs and other primates
  • Learn about medicinal plants and ecology
  • Transfer to airport for departure
