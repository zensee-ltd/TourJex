Down South

Galle • Mirissa • Unawatuna • Tangalle • Hiriketiya
cobblestone fort streets, palm-fringed bays, whale-watching mornings, and laid-back surf towns wrapped in golden sunsets.

Key Highlights & Activities

Pick your vibe! we’ll plan the flow around these experiences

Heritage

Galle Fort Exploration

Walk the 17th-century ramparts, lighthouses, and alleyway cafés inside Sri Lanka’s best-preserved coastal fort.

Ocean

Whale Watching (Mirissa)

Early-morning cruises to spot blue whales, dolphins, and flying fish off the south coast (seasonal).

Beach

Surf & Beach Time

From beginner-friendly breaks in Weligama to the crescent of Hiriketiya — chill, swim, and surf.

Nature

Turtle Conservation & Stilt Fishermen

Visit hatcheries, release hatchlings (responsibly), and catch those iconic stilt-fishing silhouettes at sunset.
