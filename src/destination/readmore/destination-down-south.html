Cultural Triangle — 3 Days

Sigiriya • Kandy • Mirissa — ancient wonders, sacred sites, and golden coasts.

Short and Sweet Getaway

A quick escape through Sri Lanka’s icons in just 3 days — Sigiriya, Kandy, and Mirissa with curated activities.

This compact itinerary blends ancient history, living heritage, and coastal calm. Ideal if you’re tight on time but want the island’s essential flavor.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Your journey through Sri Lanka’s cultural heartland

1

Arrival & Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Touch down and head straight to the lion’s fortress — a 200m monolith crowned by ancient splendor.

  • Airport pickup and transfer to Sigiriya
  • Climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress (UNESCO)
  • Royal gardens & water features
  • Frescoes of the “Heavenly Maidens”
  • Optional sunset at Pidurangala
  • Overnight in Sigiriya
2

Kandy — The Hill Capital

Wind into the hills for temples, dance, and lakeside calm in the island’s last royal city.

  • Scenic drive to Kandy via spice garden
  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Stroll around Kandy Lake
  • Traditional Kandyan dance performance
  • Markets & craft shops
  • Overnight in Kandy
3

Mirissa Beach & Departure

Finish with sea breeze and sand — or chase giants on a seasonal whale-watching cruise.

  • Early departure to Mirissa
  • Relax on the golden beach
  • Optional whale watching (seasonal)
  • Seafood lunch by the shore
  • Visit Coconut Tree Hill
  • Transfer to airport
