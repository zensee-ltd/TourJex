Hill Country Adventure

5 Days of Misty Mountains & Tea Gardens

Hill Country Paradise

Explore misty mountains, tea plantations, and scenic train journeys through Ella, Nuwara Eliya, and Kandy's highland paradise.

Journey through Sri Lanka's breathtaking hill country, where emerald tea plantations carpet rolling hills and colonial charm meets natural beauty. Experience the world-famous scenic train rides, visit working tea factories, and discover why this region is called "Little England."

Duration

5 Days / 4 Nights

Group Size

Small Groups

Price

$599.00

5-Day Hill Country Itinerary

Your journey through Sri Lanka's misty highlands

1

Arrival & Kandy Exploration

Begin your hill country adventure in the cultural capital of Kandy, nestled among misty mountains.

  • Airport pickup and scenic drive to Kandy
  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Explore Kandy Lake and surrounding gardens
  • Traditional Kandyan dance performance
  • Visit Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya
  • Overnight stay in Kandy
2

Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

Journey to "Little England" through winding mountain roads and tea-covered hills.

  • Morning departure through tea country
  • Visit a working tea factory and plantation
  • Tea tasting session with local experts
  • Arrive in Nuwara Eliya, the hill station
  • Explore colonial architecture and cool climate
  • Visit Gregory Lake and Victoria Park
  • Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya
3

Scenic Train to Ella

Experience one of the world's most beautiful train journeys through the heart of tea country.

  • Board the famous hill country train
  • Scenic journey through tea plantations
  • Photography stops at mountain viewpoints
  • Arrive in charming Ella town
  • Visit the iconic Nine Arch Bridge
  • Sunset at Little Adam's Peak
  • Overnight stay in Ella
4

Ella Adventures

Explore Ella's natural wonders, from waterfalls to mountain peaks with panoramic views.

  • Early morning hike to Ella Rock
  • Visit Ravana Falls and cave temple
  • Explore local spice gardens
  • Visit Demodara Loop and railway station
  • Traditional village lunch experience
  • Evening at leisure in Ella town
  • Overnight stay in Ella
5

Return Journey & Departure

Final morning in the hills before your journey back, with optional stops along the way.

  • Morning at leisure in Ella
  • Scenic drive back through hill country
  • Optional stop at Diyaluma Falls
  • Traditional lunch at a local restaurant
  • Transfer to airport for departure
  • End of memorable hill country adventure
