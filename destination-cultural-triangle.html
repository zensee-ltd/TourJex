Cultural Triangle Adventure

3 Days of Ancient Wonders & Sacred Sites

Short and Sweet Getaway

A quick getaway to explore Sri Lanka's iconic sights in just 3 days, featuring Sigiriya, Kandy, and Mirissa with related activities.

This carefully crafted tour takes you through Sri Lanka's most celebrated destinations, combining ancient history, spiritual heritage, and coastal beauty. Perfect for travelers with limited time who want to experience the essence of Ceylon.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Your journey through Sri Lanka's cultural heartland

1

Arrival & Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Begin your Sri Lankan adventure with the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage site rising 200 meters above the surrounding plains.

  • Airport pickup and transfer to Sigiriya
  • Climb the ancient Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore the royal gardens and water features
  • Visit the famous frescoes of the "Heavenly Maidens"
  • Sunset viewing from Pidurangala Rock (optional)
  • Overnight stay in Sigiriya
2

Kandy - Cultural Capital

Journey to Kandy, the last royal capital of Sri Lanka, nestled in the hills and home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth.

  • Morning departure to Kandy via scenic route
  • Visit a traditional spice garden
  • Explore the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Walk around the beautiful Kandy Lake
  • Traditional Kandyan dance performance
  • Shopping at Kandy's local markets
  • Overnight stay in Kandy
3

Mirissa Beach & Departure

Complete your journey with relaxation at the pristine Mirissa Beach, known for its golden sands and whale watching opportunities.

  • Early morning departure to Mirissa
  • Relax at the beautiful Mirissa Beach
  • Optional whale watching excursion (seasonal)
  • Fresh seafood lunch by the beach
  • Visit the iconic Coconut Tree Hill
  • Transfer to airport for departure
